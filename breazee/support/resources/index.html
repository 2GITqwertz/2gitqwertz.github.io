Resources and Reading

Breazee is inspired by established breathing practices and ongoing discussions around conscious breathing.

If you would like to explore the topic in more depth, the following resources provide additional background and perspectives.

The recommendations below are intended for general information and personal interest.

Breath: The New Science of a Lost Art

by James Nestor

A widely discussed book that explores the science, history, and cultural aspects of breathing.

It looks at how different breathing patterns have been practiced and studied across time and across cultures.

The book combines research, personal experiences, and interviews to provide a broad overview of how breathing techniques are approached today.
