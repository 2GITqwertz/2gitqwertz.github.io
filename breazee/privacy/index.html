Privacy Policy - Breazee

Last updated: February 15, 2025

General Information

This privacy policy explains how Breazee processes data. Protecting personal data is very important to us.

Collection and Use of Data

Breazee uses Apple Health data only if you grant the necessary permissions.

All data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transferred to external servers, third parties, or us as developers.

No Data Sharing

Breazee does not share data with third parties. The app does not use ads, tracking tools, or analytics services.

User Rights

Since no personal data is transmitted to us or third parties, your data remains solely on your device. You can manage or delete data via Apple Health at any time.

Apple Health Policies

Breazee uses health data solely for in-app functionality and not for advertising or marketing.
