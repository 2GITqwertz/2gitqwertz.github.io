How Breazee Works

Breazee is designed to guide breathing exercises directly on your Apple Watch in a calm and unobtrusive way.

The app focuses on clarity, efficiency, and privacy without distractions.

Guides Breathing on Apple Watch

Breazee guides each breathing exercise using visual cues, subtle haptic feedback, and optional audio signals.

This allows you to follow the breathing rhythm without constantly looking at the screen.

Designed for Efficiency

During active exercises, Breazee prioritizes essential information such as timing and phase changes.

Visual animations are kept intentionally simple to conserve system resources and battery life.

Health Integration

Breazee integrates with Apple Health to record breathing exercises and related data.

Privacy-First Approach

Your data remains on your device. Breazee does not share health data with third parties and follows Apple's HealthKit guidelines.
