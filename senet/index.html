Official App Description (Website Version)

This page currently focuses on the App Store description in English only.

Ancient strategy reborn

Discover Senet, one of the world's oldest known board games, reimagined for iPhone and iPad with refined visuals, immersive audio, and deeply focused tactical gameplay.

Every decision matters

Choose your side, House of Light or House of Shadow, and move your pieces across a strategic board shaped by timing, positioning, calculated risk, and momentum.

Two distinct rule systems

Classic Mode: A historically grounded and methodical strategy experience inspired by traditional interpretations.

Flow Mode: A modern evolution with faster pacing, dynamic turn structure, and smoother endgame progression.

Game features

  • Strategic board game experience
  • Local 2-player multiplayer on one device
  • Adaptive AI opponent
  • Three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard
  • Clean, readable board layout
  • Atmospheric sound design
  • Elegant turn-based mechanics
  • Designed for focused, thoughtful sessions

Who it is for

Whether you enjoy historical board games, tactical strategy games, or competitive two-player matches, Senet Reborn delivers a timeless experience with modern clarity.
