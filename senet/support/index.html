Help and Contact for Senet Reborn

Need help with Senet Reborn? This page is the central support point for questions, bug reports, and gameplay issues on iPhone and iPad.

Contact Support

For support requests, bug reports, or questions about Senet Reborn, please contact:

senetreborn@2git.de

Please include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Support languages: German and English.
Typical response time: within 2 to 5 business days.

FAQ

Which devices are supported?

Senet Reborn is designed for iPhone and iPad. Please use the compatibility information shown on the App Store product page for the latest supported OS version.

Can I play against AI?

Yes. Senet Reborn includes an AI opponent with multiple difficulty levels.

Is there a local multiplayer mode?

Yes. Two players can play on the same device.

Where can I learn the rules?

You can find the rule overview and game guidance on the How to Play page.

Report a Problem

To help identify an issue quickly, please send the following information:

  • Device model
  • iOS or iPadOS version
  • App version
  • What happened
  • What you expected
  • Whether the issue can be reproduced
  • Optional screenshot of the game state
