Privacy Policy for the iOS App "Senet Reborn"

1. Controller

The controller responsible for processing personal data in connection with the app "Senet Reborn" is the operator listed in the Imprint.

2. General Information

Protecting your personal data is important. The app "Senet Reborn" is designed to collect and store as little personal data as possible.

This privacy policy explains which data may be processed in connection with the use of the app.

3. Data Collection by the App

The app "Senet Reborn" itself:

  • does not collect personal data
  • does not store personal data
  • does not create user profiles
  • does not use its own tracking
  • does not integrate advertising services

There is no registration or login system within the app.

4. Game Center (optional, if enabled)

The app may use Apple Game Center features (for example achievements or leaderboards).

In this case, data is processed exclusively by Apple Inc. The developer does not receive access to personal Game Center data.

More information is available in Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. App Store and Device Information

When downloading and using the app, technical information may be processed by Apple as the platform operator (for example device type, operating system version, anonymous usage statistics).

This processing is carried out exclusively by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

The developer does not receive access to personal user data.

6. No Transfer to Third Parties

No personal data is shared with third parties by the developer.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If personal data is processed, you generally have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • right of access
  • right to rectification
  • right to erasure
  • right to restriction of processing
  • right to data portability
  • right to object

Since the app itself does not store personal data, such requests usually cannot be assigned to an individual user by the developer.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the contact details listed in the Imprint.
